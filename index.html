About Me

I am a dedicated educator and researcher with extensive experience in higher education, currently serving as an Assistant Professor IV at Quirino State University. My work focuses on Biology Education, Science Education, and the intersection of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education, with particular interests in AI literacy and student motivation framework modeling.
